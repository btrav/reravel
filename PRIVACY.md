# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 12, 2026

## Data collection

Reravel does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The extension runs entirely in your browser.

## What Reravel stores

Reravel uses Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage.sync` and `chrome.storage.local`) to save your preferences:

- Your list of monitored websites
- Effect settings (slider values, typography preferences)
- Your custom interstitial message
- Whether the extension is currently enabled or snoozed

This data stays in your browser and syncs across your Chrome browsers through your Google account if you have Chrome Sync enabled. Reravel never sends this data to any external server.

## Website access

Reravel requires access to monitored websites in order to inject CSS filters and display the interstitial pause screen. It does not read, collect, or transmit any content from these websites.

## Third parties

Reravel does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at https://github.com/btrav/reravel/issues.
